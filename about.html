about me

french horn

I've played french horn for five years and still don't know if I should capitalize the "f" and "h".

Peabody Wind Orchestra

Peabody Preparatory is associated with Johns Hopkins University and includes ensembles such as the Wind Orchestra, conducted by Elijah Wirth. I auditioned into it last June and will be playing with it for the 2019-2020 school year.

High School GT Orchestra

This upcoming school year will be my second year in Howard County's GT Orchestra. The ensemble is open to all Howard County high schoolers and is conducted by Benjamin Denne and Colin O'Bryan.

Centennial High School

Centennial High School has one of the most competitive band programs in the nation. I'm really excited to be moving into the Wind Ensemble this year, conducted by David Matchim.

writing

Young Author's Contest

The YAC is a writing contest available to elementary and middle school students. I competed in this every year that I could, and finally, in seventh grade, I won the school wide contest and moved on to get an honorable mention at the county level.

Our Voice

Howard County publishes an annual pamphlet with writing and art, created by middle school students and nominated by teachers. I submitted short stories that reached the pamphlet every year.
